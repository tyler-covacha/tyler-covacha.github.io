Legends of Zelda ENRAGED | September 2025

The more Link defeats enemies... The more ENRAGED HE BECOMES!!! Press "4" in-game to play a bloodthirsty Link.

Built using C# in Unity. I was tasked with:

1. Core Weapons & Items Weapons, pickups, and consumables
  • Attacks of Sword, Bow, Bomb, Boomerang
  • Collectable power-ups (Hearts, Bow/Bomb/Boomerang Collectible)
2. Environment & Level Design Rooms, tiles, puzzles, and NPCs
  • Authentic enemy & item placement + level progression
  • Pushable blocks, Water tiles
  • Goriyo's boomerang attack
  • Old-Man room with dialogue, 2D Bow room
  • Portion of custom level design: fire path room, custom boss fight
3. HUD UI Player Info
  • Health/Rupees/Keys UI - accurately keeps track of each info
  • Weapons UI - shows controls of weapons & shows which weapon is being held
